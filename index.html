About me

Postdoctoral Associate
NYU Biology



I have a background in academic research, with experience in pharmaceutical drug development, Genetics, Genomics, and Molecular and Computational Biology.

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at NYU studying meiosis, the specialized cell division required for sexual reproduction. My work involves the generation and analysis of high-throughput genome-wide sequencing data. I routinely use next-generation sequencing (NGS) technology and computational methods, including shell scripting, the programming languages R and Python, and open-source bioinformatics software.

Beyond Biology, I am interested in computational data analysis in general, or Data Science. I really enjoy studying and applying Computer Science, Math, Statistics and Machine Learning.
